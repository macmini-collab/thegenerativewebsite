Privacy Policy

Akshara Tamil Wordle · Last updated: February 22, 2026

Akshara Tamil Wordle ("Akshara", "we", "our") is a free Tamil word puzzle game. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only (never sent to us):

  • Game progress, statistics, and streak data
  • App preferences (dark mode, language, notifications)

This data is stored locally using SharedPreferences on your device. We do not have access to it and it is never transmitted to any server.

Anonymous analytics (via Firebase):

  • App usage events (e.g., game started, puzzle completed, settings changed)
  • Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us understand how the app is used and fix bugs.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Account or login information (no account is required)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics – anonymous usage analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics – anonymous crash reporting. Crashlytics Terms

These services are provided by Google and are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Local Notifications

Akshara may send local notifications (e.g., daily puzzle reminders) if you allow them. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external server. You can disable them at any time in the app's settings or your device's notification settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Akshara is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

6. Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can clear it at any time by uninstalling the app. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Firebase according to Google's standard retention policies.

7. Your Choices

  • Notifications: You can disable daily reminders in the app settings or your device settings.
  • Analytics: You can limit ad tracking and analytics collection through your device's privacy settings.
  • Delete data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
