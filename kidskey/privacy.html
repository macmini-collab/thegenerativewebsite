Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

KidsKey Piano ("we", "our", or "the app") is a children's piano learning app. We take your child's privacy very seriously. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

KidsKey Piano is designed to operate without collecting personal information. Specifically:

  • No account required. The app does not require registration, login, or any personal details.
  • No personal data collected. We do not collect names, email addresses, photos, or location data.
  • All data stored locally. Game progress, preferences, and settings are stored only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Microphone Access

The app may request access to your device's microphone to detect piano notes played on a physical piano. Microphone audio is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

KidsKey Piano does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

This app is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. Because no data is collected or transmitted, the app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
